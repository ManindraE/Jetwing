About Us

Founded in 1972 by Herbert Cooray, the company has a long and rich history of providing guests with a warm and hospitable welcome. The company has won numerous awards for its sustainability initiatives, including the World Travel Awards' "Best Hotel Chain for Sustainability in Sri Lanka" in 2022. Jetwing Hotels is also a strong supporter of local communities, and actively engages with them through a variety of programs.The company's mission is to "create memorable experiences that leave a lasting impression." Jetwing Hotels achieves this through its focus on providing excellent service, luxurious accommodations, and authentic Sri Lankan experiences.
