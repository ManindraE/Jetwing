Our Hotels

Jetwing Colombo Seven

Jetwing Colombo Seven is a luxurious hotel located in the heart of Colombo, Sri Lanka. The hotel is built on the land of the founder's bygone residence, and it retains a sense of family heritage throughout its modern facilities.The hotel has 98 rooms, including 54 Deluxe and 16 Super Deluxe Rooms. All of the rooms are decorated in a modern minimalist style, and they offer invitingly comfortable accommodations. The hotel also has two three-bedroom Serviced Apartments and five two-bedroom Serviced Apartments.The hotel also has a number of other facilities, including a spa, infinity pool, gym, and business center. Jetwing Colombo Seven is the perfect place to stay for business or leisure travelers alike.

Jetwing Kandy Gallery

Jetwing Kandy Gallery is a luxury boutique hotel located in the heart of Kandy, Sri Lanka. The hotel is set on 15 acres of lush gardens overlooking the Mahaweli River, and it offers stunning views of the surrounding hills.The hotel has 81 rooms, including 60 Deluxe Rooms, 15 Luxury Rooms, and 6 Gallery Suites. All of the rooms are decorated in a traditional Sri Lankan style, and they offer comfortable accommodations with all the modern amenities.

Jetwing Jaffna

Jetwing Jaffna is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Jaffna, Sri Lanka. The hotel is set on 40 perches of land on the historical Mahatma Gandhi Road, and it offers stunning views of the city. The hotel has 55 rooms, including 40 Deluxe Rooms, 10 Peninsula Rooms, and 5 Jaffna Sky Suites. All of the rooms are decorated in a contemporary Sri Lankan style, and they offer comfortable accommodations with all the modern amenities. The hotel also has a number of other facilities, including a spa, infinity pool, gym, and business center. Jetwing Jaffna is the perfect place to stay for business or for Vacation.
