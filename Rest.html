Rancher's Table

The Rancher's Table is a Western-themed restaurant, the restaurant features a warm and inviting atmosphere with exposed beams, wagon wheel chandeliers, and Western artwork. The menu features classic American cuisine with a Western twist, such as grilled steaks, roasted chicken, and fresh seafood. The restaurant also offers a wide selection of wines and cocktails.
The Rancher's Table is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious meal with friends or family. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner or a casual meal with colleagues, The Rancher's Table is the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a taste of the West.

The menu features authentic Sri Lankan cuisine, such as rice and curry, kottu roti, and hoppers. The restaurant also offers a wide selection of Sri Lankan beers, wines, and spirits.
The Spice Merchant is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious and authentic Sri Lankan meal. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner or a casual meal with friends or family, The Spice Merchant is the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a taste of Sri Lanka.
